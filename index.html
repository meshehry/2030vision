PHASE 3 · Outreach + Activation
Reach solo physicians.
Become their team.
With services built and partnerships formalized, Phase 3 turns the Population Health Clinic outward — actively outreach to solo physicians and start providing the team-based services they cannot offer alone.
Phase 3 — what we offer solo MDs
The PHC becomes the solo physician's team
Solo physicians cannot offer interdisciplinary care alone. The PHC provides exactly that — wrap-around services that strengthen their panel, not replace it.
01
Active outreach
PHC navigation team contacts solo physicians directly. Maps their panel needs. Builds trust. No top-down mandate — invitation-based onboarding.
02
Shared care pathway
Solo MD retains attachment. PHC provides home visits, paramedicine, OH at Home, mental health — all the services the solo MD cannot do alone.
03
Population health services
PHC runs screening, preventive, and chronic disease programs on behalf of solo MD panels. Pap clinic, diabetes group, immunizations.
04
Complex patient uptake
PHC absorbs complex patients during stabilization, then transitions back to solo MD with shared care plan. Capacity multiplier.
05
Workforce transition support
Retiring solo MDs use PHC + PCN registry for orderly panel transition. No patients orphaned.
06
Data + accountability
PHC provides population health reports to solo MDs — screening gaps, complex patient lists, equity outcomes. Data they cannot generate alone.
